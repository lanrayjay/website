About us

Welcome to Minds & Souls

Minds & Souls Foundation is an organization dedicated to helping needy children in the African continent. The aim is to ensure that the less privileged are given equal opportunities by providing them education. Our goal is to eradicate illiteracy in Africa- one child, one community and one country at a time- to preserve their innocence and giving them a chance to have dreams.

The Minds and Souls Foundation is hereby providing a donation platform, in addition to humanitarian support from the general public, to assist in providing the basic schooling amenities to educate an African child, and making him become a Leader in a future that is not too distant.
